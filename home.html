What is a Healthy Lifestyle? Its a way of living that LOWERS THE RISK of being seriously ill or dying early. Not all illness and disease is preventable; however a large proportion of deaths, particularly those from coronary heart disease and lung cancer, can be avoided. But I would like to think of a healthy lifestyle as more than that. It should nuture, inspire and motivate everything and everyone it comes into contact with.
