Here are a few ways suggested to improve Brain Health.

  • Get mental stimulation Scientists have found that brainy activities stimulate new connections between nerve cells and may even help the brain generate new cells, developing neurological "plasticity" and building up a functional reserve that provides a hedge against future cell loss.
  • Consider low-dose aspirinSome observational studies suggest that low-dose aspirin may reduce the risk of dementia, especially vascular dementia. Ask your doctor if you are a candidate.
  • Build social networksStrong social ties have been associated with a lower risk of dementia, as well as lower blood pressure and longer life expectancy.

A healthy mind includes emotional, psychological and social well-being and affects how you think, feel and act. It works in unity with your body and influences how you handle stress, relate to others and make choices.
